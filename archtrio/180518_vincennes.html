Vincennes Downtown
Revitalization Plan

Update & Bid Notice

Bid Date - June 11, 2018

Existing Conditions

Things are happening in the downtown area of Vincennes and a big step toward bringing back the historic downtown of Vincennes. The body passed the Downtown Revitalization Plan unanimously. Funding for the study was partially provided by a federal block grant from the Indiana Office of Community and Rural Affairs as well as with building participants. Brian O'Neill of SDG says, "When you create an amenity like a beautiful trail or a park, the real estate around those amenities becomes more valuable. Investors and developers are more interested in housing and retail opportunities in places that provide an amenity like that that attracts people. Mayor Joe Yochum says, "Things are really happening with the downtown and it's exciting. This just gives us some other thoughts and ideas and things to look at for our downtown."
