About
ARCHitecture trio

Certified Women's Business Enterprise (WBE) in the State of Indiana

ARCHitecture trio, inc. is one of the Midwest's foremost contextual design firms, utilizing the existing environment to create compatible, sympathetic design solutions for preservation, renovation, new construction & additions. We focus on analysis, insight & respect for how the context influences & is influenced by our designs. Combining formal education, experience & complimentary partnerships with like-minded consultants, we are able to accomplish your project goals.
